Trip Cost Calculation Details
=============================

Total Amount Calculation
------------------------

1. Fuel Cost:
   Formula: (Distance / Avg KM per Litre) x Fuel Price per Litre
   
   Example:
   - Distance: 350 km
   - Avg KM/L: 14 km/L (depends on vehicle selected)
   - Fuel Price: 275 PKR/L
   
   Fuel Cost = (350 / 14) x 275 = 6,875 PKR

2. Extra Costs (Toll & Parking + Food & Snacks):
   - Toll & Parking: User enters manually (e.g., 500 PKR)
   - Food & Snacks: User enters manually (e.g., 1,000 PKR)
   
   Extra Costs = 500 + 1,000 = 1,500 PKR

3. Total Amount:
   Total Amount = Fuel Cost + Extra Costs
   
   Example:
   Total Amount = 6,875 + 1,500 = 8,375 PKR

4. Per Person Cost:
   Per Person = Total Amount / Number of People
   
   Example (with 4 people):
   Per Person = 8,375 / 4 = 2,094 PKR (rounded)


Vehicle Presets
-----------
The app comes with pre-defined vehicles that have their own fuel efficiency:

- Alto: 18 km/L
- Passo: 16 km/L
- Civic: 14 km/L
- City: 13 km/L
- Corolla: 13 km/L
- Swift: 16 km/L
- WagonR: 16 km/L
- Mehran: 18 km/L
- Cultus: 16 km/L
- Other: 14 km/L

Default fuel price: 275 PKR/L for all vehicles


User Inputs Required
-------------------
1. Route Details:
   - From location
   - To location
   - Distance (in km)

2. Vehicle Selection:
   - Select a vehicle from presets

3. Extra Costs:
   - Toll & Parking (optional)
   - Food & Snacks (optional)

4. Number of People:
   - Minimum 1 person
